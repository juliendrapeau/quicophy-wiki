# Tropical Tensor Networks

**Ising model:** 
$$H(\sigma) = \sum_{i<j} J_{ij} \sigma_{i} \sigma_{j} - \sum_{i} h_{i} \sigma_{i}$$

**Tropical Algebra:** 
$$\begin{align}
x \oplus y &= \max (x,y) \\
x \otimes y &= x + y \\
\implies & \text{ Zero element } \to - \infty \\
\implies & \text{ Multiplicative identity } \to 0 
\end{align}$$
